Frequently Asked Questions

What is a hackathon and why should I attend?

A hackathon is an event where people come together to create solutions to real world problems using technology. It provides an excellent opportunity to shape your ideas into prototypes and observe their viability. You will also meet many people who share your passions!

Who can attend?

PeddieHacks is open to all high schoolers.

Do I need to have programming experience?

Absolutely not! This hackathon is for both novice and experienced programmers who want to apply their skills to solve real problems in the world. You can easily learn some programming on the spot or contribute to your team through graphic design, interface design, etc.

How much does it cost?

Nothing! This hackathon is free to all high schoolers, all you need to bring is ideas and a postive attitude. All gifts and resources are free of charge courtesy of our wonderful sponsors!

Yes! We have over $10,000 in prizes and over $500 in raffle prizes that anyone can win! Check out the Prizes page for more information!
